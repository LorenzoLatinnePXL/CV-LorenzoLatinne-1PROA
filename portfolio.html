Intro

Welcome to My Portfolio!

Hi, I’m Lorenzo Latinne, an aspiring software and web developer with a passion for learning and creating. I’m currently pursuing an Associate Degree in Programming and honing my skills in C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. This portfolio serves as both a final project and a showcase of my growth.

Here, you’ll find highlights of my work, including projects like a Mastermind game built in C# and this portfolio itself. Each project reflects my enthusiasm for blending logic and creativity—a hallmark of programming that I find deeply rewarding.

Before diving into IT, I gained valuable experience as a Manager’s Assistant, where I sharpened my organizational and communication skills. Outside of coding, I enjoy spending time with my wife and friends, playing guitar, and exploring the balance of discipline and creativity through music.

Thanks for visiting—I’m excited to share my journey and future projects with you!
